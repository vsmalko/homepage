Experience

    Became a Gryffindor student and the youngest seeker in a faculty’s quidditch team in 100 years, saved Hermione Granger from the troll, bypassed the three-headed dog, Devil’s Snare, winged keys, the mountain troll, potion riddle and the mirror of erased to get to the Philosopher’s Stone.     Discovered that I can speak Parseltongue, read Tom Riddle’s diary, talked to Aragog, the king of spiders, disarmed professor Lockhart who faked his way into Hogwarts, saved Jinny Weasley from Tom Riddle and the Basilisk, killed Basilisk with a sword and a help of the Sorting Hat and Phoenix, destroyed Tom Riddle’s ghost and his diary, freed Dobby from Lucius Malfoy.

    Learned Expecto Patronum as a protection against dementors, won the Quidditch cup, used Time-Turner to save innocent Hyppofriddf from unfair execution and help Sirius Black escape.

    Helped Sirius Black in exile, took part in the Triwizard Tournament: got the dragon egg, saved three hostages from underwater creatures, solved the sphinx’s puzzle and went through the magical maze; won the Goblet of Fire; yet again survived facing Voldemort; exchanged important information about the rise of Voldemort and The Death Eaters with Hogwarts executives.

  • 1995-1996, the fifth year

    Fought Dementors to save Dudleys, joined the members of the Order of Phoenix to battle the Death in the Second Wizarding War.

  • 1996-1997, the sixth year

    Was appointed as the Gryffindor Quidditch Captain; won the Quidditch Cup.

  • 1997-1998, the last year

    Found all the Deathly Hallows and killed Lord Voldemort.

  • 1998–2006

    At the end of the Second Wizarding War in 1995, was recruited by the new Minister for Magic Kingsley Shacklebolt and became an Auror at the Ministry of Magic.

    After graduating, occasionally gave lectures on Defence Against the Dark Arts at Hogwarts.

  • 2007–

    Became the Head of the Auror Office at the age of 26 or 27.

    Was the leader of the members of the Statute of Secrecy Task Force, an emergency response team established by the International Confederation of Wizards with the help of the British Ministry of Magic.

    to deal with large-scale breaches of the International Statute of Wizarding Secrecy.

    By late summer of 2020, was made Head of the Department of Magical Law Enforcement.
